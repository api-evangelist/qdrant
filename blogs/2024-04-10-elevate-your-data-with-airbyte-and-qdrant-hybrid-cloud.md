In their mission to support large-scale AI innovation, Airbyte and Qdrant are collaborating on the launch of Qdrant’s new offering - Qdrant Hybrid Cloud . This collaboration allows users to leverage the synergistic capabilities of both Airbyte and Qdrant within a private infrastructure. Qdrant’s new offering represents the first managed vector database that can be deployed in any environment.
