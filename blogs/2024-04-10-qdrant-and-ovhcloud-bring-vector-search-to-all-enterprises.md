<p>With the official release of <a href="https://qdrant.tech/hybrid-cloud/">Qdrant Hybrid Cloud</a>, businesses running their data infrastructure on <a href="https://ovhcloud.com/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">OVHcloud</a> are now able to deploy a fully managed vector database in their existing OVHcloud environment. We are excited about this partnership, which has been established through the <a href="https://opentrustedcloud.ovhcloud.com/en/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">OVHcloud Open Trusted Cloud</a> program, as it is based on our shared understanding of the importance of trust, control, and data privacy in the context of the emerging landscape of enterprise-grade AI applications. As part of this collaboration, we are also providing a detailed use case tutorial on building a recommendation system that demonstrates the benefits of running Qdrant Hybrid Cloud on OVHcloud.</p>
