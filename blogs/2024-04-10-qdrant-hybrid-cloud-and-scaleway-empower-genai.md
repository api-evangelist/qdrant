<p>In a move to empower the next wave of AI innovation, Qdrant and <a href="https://www.scaleway.com/en/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Scaleway</a> collaborate to introduce <a href="https://qdrant.tech/hybrid-cloud/">Qdrant Hybrid Cloud</a>, a fully managed vector database that can be deployed on existing Scaleway environments. This collaboration is set to democratize access to advanced AI capabilities, enabling developers to easily deploy and scale vector search technologies within Scaleway&rsquo;s robust and developer-friendly cloud infrastructure. By focusing on the unique needs of startups and the developer community, Qdrant and Scaleway are providing access to intuitive and easy to use tools, making cutting-edge AI more accessible than ever before.</p>
