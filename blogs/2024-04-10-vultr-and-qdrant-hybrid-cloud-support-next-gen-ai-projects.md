<p>We’re excited to share that Qdrant and <a href="https://www.vultr.com/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Vultr</a> are partnering to provide seamless scalability and performance for vector search workloads. With Vultr&rsquo;s global footprint and customizable platform, deploying vector search workloads becomes incredibly flexible. Qdrant&rsquo;s new <a href="https://qdrant.tech/hybrid-cloud/">Qdrant Hybrid Cloud</a> offering and its Kubernetes-native design, coupled with Vultr&rsquo;s straightforward virtual machine provisioning, allows for simple setup when prototyping and building next-gen AI apps.</p>
<h4 id="adapting-to-diverse-ai-development-needs-with-customization-and-deployment-flexibility">Adapting to Diverse AI Development Needs with Customization and Deployment Flexibility</h4>
<p>In the fast-paced world of AI and ML, businesses are eagerly integrating AI and generative AI to enhance their products with new features like AI assistants, develop new innovative solutions, and streamline internal workflows with AI-driven processes. Given the diverse needs of these applications, it&rsquo;s clear that a one-size-fits-all approach doesn&rsquo;t apply to AI development. This variability in requirements underscores the need for adaptable and customizable development environments.</p>
