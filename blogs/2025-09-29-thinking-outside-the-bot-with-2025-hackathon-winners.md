Over the past several weeks, builders from around the world proved that vector search is about much more than chatbots. We challenged teams to think beyond RAG, and they delivered: robotics safety reflexes, event discovery on routes, 3D shopping, video game characters, and more. Winners were announced live in Berlin on Friday, September 26, 2025 at Vector Space Day .
