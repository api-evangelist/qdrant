Cosmos is redefining how people find inspiration online. It’s a visual search app built for creative professionals and everyday users who want a clean, meditative, ad-free place to collect and curate ideas. In contrast to feeds dominated by doomscrolling, ads, and generative “AI slop,” Cosmos focuses on high-quality, human-made content.
