Qdrant 1.17.0 is out! Let’s look at the main features for this version: Relevance Feedback Query: Improve the quality of search results by incorporating information about their relevance. Search Latency Improvements: Manage search latency with new tools, such as an update queue and delayed fan-outs, as well as many internal search performance improvements.
