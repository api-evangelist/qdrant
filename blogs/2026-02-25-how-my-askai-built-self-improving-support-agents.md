My AskAI built a managed platform for AI customer support agents that plug directly into existing helpdesk tools like Intercom and Zendesk . The goal was to make AI behave like a reliable coworker, not a brittle chatbot. In production, My AskAI’s agents are designed to resolve a large portion of inbound support requests automatically, then hand over to a human when the agent cannot answer confidently.
